Document Detail Page

View a document's details such as name, status, and revision date.

  • To edit the document information, click Edit, make the necessary revisions, and click Save.
  • To duplicate the information, click Duplicate. You can then make modifications to the record and save it as a different document. You can then make modifications to the record and save it as a different record. The system displays the new record in the list on the Documents Home page.
  • To remove a document from the list, click Delete.
  • To add a new revision, click Create in the Document Revisions sub-panel and, in the Document Revision page, do the following:
    • In the Filename field, enter the file path, or click Browse to navigate to the file location.
    • In the Revision field, enter the revision number.
    • In the Change Log field, enter a brief description of the document revision.
    • Click Save to add the new revision.
