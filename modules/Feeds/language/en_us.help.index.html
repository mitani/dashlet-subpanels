RSS News Feeds

Use the RSS module to create and manage your RDF Site Summary (RSS) feeds.

The RSS Home page displays the following information:

  • A list of news feeds along with the timestamp of when the item was added.
  • A Shortcuts section that displays the following options:
    • My RSS News Feeds. Click this option to view your bookmarked news feeds.
    • All RSS News Feeds. Click this option to view a list of all RSS News Feeds from a recent search
  • A New RSS News Feed quick form to add a new news feed.
