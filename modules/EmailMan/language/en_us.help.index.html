Outbound Email Queue Management

Use this page to view and manage mass campaign emails that are in the queue for dispatch.

This page displays the following information:

  • A Search sub-panel to search for a specific campaign using the campaign name, the recipient's name or email address.
  • A list of existing email queues. Click Send Queued Campaign Emails to dispatch all queued campaigns in the list.
  • A Shortcuts menu that displays the following option:
      View Queued Emails. Click this option to navigate back to the Outbound Email Queue Managment page from a campaign's Detail View.
To delete one or more emails in the queue, select them from the list and click Delete.
