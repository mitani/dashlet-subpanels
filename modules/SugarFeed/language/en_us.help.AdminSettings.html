Sugar Feed Settings

Use this page to enable and disable Sugar Feeds for your organization. Sugar Feed enables users to be informed within the My Sugar Feed dashlet as soon as a team member creates a new contact, lead, opportunity, or case. Team members are also notified when a lead is converted and when a case is closed.

  • Enable Sugar Feed. Select this option to enable Sugar feeds for your organization.
  • Activate User Feed. Select this option to enable users to post their status along with YouTube videos, links, and images.
  • Activate Feeds for. To activate Sugar feeds for a module, select the appropriate checkbox. You can activate feeds for Leads, Cases, Opportunities, and Contacts.
Click Save to save the settings; click Cancel to exit the page without making any changes.

Click Delete Feed Entries to delete all existing entries within Sugar Feed. Sugar Feed entries are automatically deleted every two weeks.
